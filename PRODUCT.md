# Product

## Register

product

## Users

CapitalFlow is for a private self-hosting user who wants a reliable personal finance center. The user works with accounts, transactions, transfers, interest rules, savings, deposits, and future investment data. They need to understand current money state quickly, then inspect exact records when something looks wrong.

## Product Purpose

CapitalFlow tracks personal finances with explainable balances. It helps answer how much money exists now, where it is stored, what changed recently, how income and spending moved this month, and whether each balance can be audited later. Success means the user can trust the ledger, trace every balance-changing event, and run the app without cloud or SaaS assumptions.

## Brand Personality

Calm, exact, private. The interface should feel like a practical daily finance tool: compact, readable, restrained, and confident. It should avoid spectacle and keep attention on money, dates, accounts, categories, source, and status.

## Anti-references

CapitalFlow must not look like a marketing landing page, a crypto trading terminal, a gamified budget app, a glassmorphism showcase, or a banking clone with large decorative hero sections. Avoid decorative gradients, heavy blur, loud color, hidden money actions, and SaaS-style upsell patterns.

## Design Principles

1. Put balance and recent change first.
2. Make financial data dense but readable.
3. Keep frequent actions fast through command, search, shortcuts, and compact overlays.
4. Make detail views auditable: source, relation, status, and record identity should be easy to find.
5. Preserve predictability: money-changing actions must be explicit, reversible where possible, and never surprising.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target accessible product UI with visible labels, clear focus rings, keyboard-operable dialogs and tables, reduced-motion-friendly interaction, and color-independent status communication. Mobile layouts must work from 320px upward, while desktop layouts must remain scannable at 1280px and wider.
